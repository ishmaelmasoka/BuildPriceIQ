Supplier Guide

How to Join

  1. Click "Register" and select "Supplier" account type
  2. Fill in your business details (name, location, phone)
  3. Submit for admin approval (takes 24-48 hours)
  4. Once approved, you can start adding prices

How to Add Prices

  1. Login to your supplier account
  2. Go to the Supplier Dashboard
  3. Enter material name, price, and stock quantity
  4. Click "Add Product" button
  5. Your price will appear in search results immediately

Tips for Success

  • Keep your prices competitive
  • Update prices regularly as market changes
  • Respond quickly to quote requests
  • Provide accurate contact information
  • Keep stock information up to date
