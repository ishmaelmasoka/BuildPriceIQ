About BuildPriceIQ

Who We Are

BuildPriceIQ is a price intelligence platform designed specifically for the Zimbabwean construction industry. We help contractors, builders, homeowners and suppliers connect through transparent pricing and data-driven insights.

Our mission is to eliminate price guesswork and save you money on construction materials by providing real-time price comparisons, project cost estimates and market trend analysis.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BuildPriceIQ

❓ How does BuildPriceIQ work?
BuildPriceIQ collects real-time price data from verified suppliers across Zimbabwe. You can search for any construction material, compare prices from different suppliers, get project cost estimates and track market trends over time.
💰 Is BuildPriceIQ free to use?
Yes! BuildPriceIQ is completely free for customers. You can search, compare and estimate costs without paying anything. Suppliers can join for free and only pay a small commission when they make a sale through our platform.
🏪 How do I become a supplier?
Simply click "Register" and select "Supplier" as your account type. Fill in your business details and our team will verify your information within 12 hours. Once approved, you can start adding your prices immediately.
📊 How accurate are price predictions?
Our price predictions are based on historical data, supplier's actual prices and market trends. While we strive for accuracy, actual prices may vary due to some other market conditions. We recommend using predictions as a guide rather than a guarantee.
🛡️ Is my data secure?
Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal information. Passwords are hashed and we never share your data with third parties without your consent.
📍 Which cities do you cover?
Currently, BuildPriceIQ covers Harare, Bulawayo, Mutare and Gweru. We're expanding to Masvingo, Kwekwe, Kadoma and other cities soon. Stay tuned!
